Projects

First Principles Study of Hydrogen Storage in Transition-Metal doped Carbon Nanostructures
High Pressure & Synchrotron Radiation Physics Division, BARC
This ongoing project is being conducted under the supervision of Dr. B. Chakraborty, Scientist G of HP & SRPD, BARC. The determination of Hydrogen Storage Capabilities of various new materials in accordance with the DOE's standards is being analyzed using BARC's supercomputing facility.

Determination of Water Contamination Content Profile in a Steel Plant Belt (Durgapur)
Physics Department, NIT Durgapur
 This ongoing project is being conducted under the supervision of Prof. Hirok Chaudhari. The determination of the content profile and their effect in our daily lives, besides a few other detrimental physical factors are measured.

Identification of Post Investment Casting Defects
Development Department, F&F Division, Hindustan Aeronautics Limited
 The project aims at the identification of various defects that can be detected post-casting only like microshrinkage, blowholes, etc., their analysis and finally their mitigation.

Preventive Measures for Quality Control using Lean Management
Quality Control Department, F&F Division, Hindustan Aeronautics Limited
 The project aims at designing of Fish-Bone (Fishikawa) Diagrams to mitigate the errors caused during various sectors of the casting process used to manufacture various aircraft parts, with materials such as Mn-alloys, Al-alloys, etc..
